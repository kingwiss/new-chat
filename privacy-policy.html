Privacy Policy

Cerebray Video Chat Platform

Effective Date: December 2024

Your Privacy Matters: Cerebray is designed with privacy in mind. We do not store personal conversations, video recordings, or personal data on our servers.

1. Information We Collect

Cerebray collects minimal information to provide our video chat service:

  • Technical Information: Browser type, IP address, and connection data for service functionality
  • Usage Data: Anonymous statistics about service usage and performance
  • Preference Settings: Your chat preferences (gender, category) stored locally in your browser
  • Contact Form Data: Information you voluntarily provide when contacting us

We DO NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Video or audio recordings
  • Chat conversation content
  • Photos or personal files

2. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Facilitate video chat connections between users
  • Maintain and improve service performance
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse
  • Respond to user inquiries and support requests
  • Generate anonymous usage statistics

3. Data Storage and Security

No Conversation Storage: Cerebray does not record, store, or save any video calls, audio, or text messages exchanged between users.

Security measures we implement:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted
  • WebRTC Security: Direct peer-to-peer connections for video/audio
  • No Server Recording: Conversations are not routed through or stored on our servers
  • Temporary Sessions: Connection data is deleted when sessions end

4. Cookies and Local Storage

Cerebray uses minimal browser storage:

  • Preference Cookies: To remember your chat preferences
  • Session Data: Temporary data for maintaining connections
  • No Tracking: We do not use tracking cookies or analytics that identify you personally

You can clear this data anytime through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Cerebray may use third-party services for:

  • Hosting: Platform hosting and content delivery
  • Contact Forms: Processing contact form submissions
  • Security: Protection against abuse and spam

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and our data processing agreements.

6. Age Requirements

Cerebray is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors under 18. If we become aware that a minor has provided information, we will take steps to remove such information.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we may have
  • Deletion: Request deletion of any stored data
  • Correction: Correct any inaccurate information
  • Opt-out: Stop using the service at any time

8. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Session Data: Deleted immediately when you disconnect
  • Technical Logs: Kept for up to 30 days for security purposes
  • Contact Inquiries: Kept until resolved, then deleted
  • No Long-term Storage: We do not maintain long-term user profiles

9. International Users

Cerebray is accessible globally. By using our service, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in accordance with this privacy policy, regardless of your location.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the effective date and posting the new policy on our website.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through:

  • The contact form on our main website
  • Our support channels

We will respond to privacy-related inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.

12. Commitment to Privacy

Our Promise: Cerebray is committed to protecting your privacy. We believe in transparent communication and minimal data collection. Your conversations are private and not monitored, recorded, or stored by us.
